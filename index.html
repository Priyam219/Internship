TESTIMONIAL

Observed every year on May 3rd, Wild Koala Day was established by concerned koala conservationists across Australia to celebrate this species and to protect their habitats.
As solitary creatures, koalas stick to their home ranges and generally respect the boundaries of others.
Each front paw has 5 digits, two of which are opposed (similar to our thumbs) and able to move separately from the others.
